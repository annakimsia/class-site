Burrata Salad Pasta

Ingredients

Recipe

* for 1 serving
Before you begin, let your burrata sit at room temp for 20 to 30 minutes
1. Boil 80g of pasta on high for 7 minutes with 1T coarse salt
2. While boiling, combine arugula and tomatoes in a bowl
3. Add 1T olive oil + salt + pepper and mix lightly
4. In a pan on medium heat, add 1T olive oil, 1/2 minced garlic, 1t crushed red pepper
5. When garlic is golden, add pasta + 3T pasta water.
6. Add 1T garlic granule and cook 1 to 2 minutes more
7. Plate pasta and add your salad mixture
8. Top with burrata, olive oil, and pepper
ENJOY!
