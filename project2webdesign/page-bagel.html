Ricotta Bagel Sandwich

Ingredients

Recipe

* for 1 serving
1. In a mixing bowl, add 1 cup of ricotta cheese, 2T of chopped walnuts, and 1T of honey
2. Mix and set aside
3. Slice your bagel in half, horizontally, and lightly toast
4. Assemble in this order: 1/2 bagel, arugula, 3 to 4 thinly sliced tomato, 3-4 slices of ham, 2 slices of creamy slice cheese, ricotta cheese mixture, 1/2 bagel
5. cut your bagel sandwich in half, vertially
ENJOY!
