Burrata Salad Pasta

Instructions

*You will need: A handful of Arugula, 3-4 slices of Tomato, 4 slices of Ham, 1 cup of Ricotta, 1/3 cup of chopped Walnuts, 3 swirls of Honey. 1. combine 1 cup of Ricotta Cheese, 1/3 cup of chopped Walnuts, 3 swirls of Honey in mixing bowl
2. Mix well and set it aside
3. Slice a tomato in 1/2 inch thickness 4. slice your bagel in half, horizontally
4. Assemble in the following order: Bottom of the Bagel, Argula, Tomato, Ham, Ricotta Mix, Top of the Bagel
